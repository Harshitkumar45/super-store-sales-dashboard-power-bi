# 🛒 Super Store Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Super Store Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profit, customer segments, payment methods, shipping modes, and regional sales trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, helping stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions.

This project demonstrates data visualization, business intelligence, and dashboard development skills using Microsoft Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Compare sales across different product categories.
- Monitor monthly sales and profit trends.
- Identify top-performing states and regions.
- Analyze customer segments and payment methods.
- Understand shipping mode preferences.
- Support strategic business decisions using interactive dashboards.

## 📍 Key Insights

### 🛒 Sales Analysis
- Total sales reached **341.01K**.
- Office Supplies generated the highest sales.
- Technology ranked third among all product categories.

### 👥 Customer Segment
- Consumer segment contributed **48%** of total sales.
- Corporate customers accounted for **33%**.
- Home Office customers represented **20%**.

### 💳 Payment Mode
- COD was the most preferred payment method (**41%**).
- Online payments accounted for **39%**.
- Card payments contributed **20%**.

### 🚚 Shipping Mode
- Standard Class generated the highest sales (**78K**).
- Second Class contributed **32K**.
- First Class generated **16K**.
- Same Day shipping accounted for **7K**.

### 📈 Sales Trend
- Sales increased significantly during the last quarter.
- December recorded the highest monthly sales.

### 💰 Profit Trend
- Profit fluctuated throughout the year.
- October showed the highest monthly profit.

### 🌎 Geographic Analysis
- The dashboard includes an interactive map displaying state-wise sales across the United States.
- Users can identify high-performing states using map bubbles.
